Shivoham - A Cosmic Identity

Meaning

The word Shivoham from Sanskrit indicates a cosmic identity. In yogic lore, the word Shi-va indicates 'no-thing', beyond physical, a certain emptiness. It is this emptiness that binds the entire universe. It is this emptiness that pervades every atom. So Shivoham means to move from a limited identity to the cosmic identity.
