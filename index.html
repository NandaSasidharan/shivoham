    Why Classical Hatha Yoga?

    Yoga is a profound technology for exploring one's inner nature. It opens up the possibility of experiencing life at a deeper level, which most of us would otherwise miss. But today, in many parts of the world, Yoga is practiced as a group event. While it might give some solace and relaxation from stressful life and physical relief from desk jobs, it fails to serve its original purpose.

    We offer Classical Hatha Yoga, which has survived many millennia simply due to its efficacy, in its original form without any modifications. Sadhguru's insights and guidance ensure the transmission of this technology as a tool for inner exploration. They are designed to create an inner balance, raise your energies, and bring clarity in life. Since my journey started in 2021, Yoga has made my life a love affair.

    Yogic practices we offer

    They are taught not merely for physical fitness and strength!

    Upa-Yoga

    A simple yet powerful set of 10 practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, bringing ease to the whole system.

    Surya Shakti

    Energize the system to a different dimension. This 18-step process helps to strengthen the ligaments that hold the skeletal and muscular structure together.

    Angamardana

    A series of 31 dynamic processes, rooted in yoga, to invigorate the body and reach peak physical fitness and mental health.

    Surya Kriya

    A potent 21-step yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health and inner wellbeing.

    Yogasanas

    Yogasanas are a set of powerful postures through which one can elevate one's consciousness and energies.

    Bhutha Shuddhi

    Bhuta Shuddhi means purification of the five elements, i.e. earth, water, fire, air and space, which gets to root of imbalances in the body that lead to disease.
