How to make Hatha Yoga part of your life?

Classical Hatha Yoga practices are taught in a few sessions conducted over consecutive days. Once learned you can practice them wherever you are. Regular practice of Hatha Yoga can transform you in many ways.

  • Balances the Autonomic Nervous System
  • Gastrointestinal function normalizes
  • Cardiovascular efficiency increases
  • Respiratory efficiency increases
  • Endocrine function normalizes
  • Musculoskeletal flexibility
  • Excretory functions improve
  • Weight normalizes
  • Energy level increases
  • Anxiety and Depression decreases
  • Attention, Concentration improves
  • Mood improves and subjective well-being increases

The Yogic Practices

Upa-Yoga, Angamardana, Surya Shakti, Surya Kriya, Yogasanas, Bhuta Shuddhi are the main practices that are offered. Anyone can make use of them to gradually upgrade their system. They require no eqipment or prior experience.

Hatha Yoga stems from a deep understanding of the mechanics of the body, and uses yogic postures, or yogasanas, to enable the system to sustain higher levels of energy. By practicing this profound science, one can change and enhance the way one thinks, feels, and experiences life.

Upa-Yoga

Upa-yoga is a simple yet powerful set of 10 practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, bringing ease to the whole system.

Upa-yoga is start-up yoga. It is best for casual practitioners seeking higher levels of physical and psychological wellbeing. - Sadhguru

Time to learn: 4-5 hrs

Time to practice: ~40 min

Anagamardana

Angamardana is a series of 31 processes to invigorate the body, and reach peak physical fitness and mental health.

Angamardana is a beautiful way of preparing the human mechanism for a higher possibility. Above all, it is one of the simplest and most powerful ways to gain fitness because it does not need any equipment or outside support. - Sadhguru

Time to learn: 11-14 hrs

Time to practice: ~40 min

Surya Shakti

Surya Shakti is to energize the system to a different dimension. This 18-step process helps to strengthen the ligaments that hold the skeletal and muscular structure together.

Surya Kriya

Surya Kriya is a potent 21-step yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health and inner wellbeing.

One should approach Surya Kriya with devotion and reverence - it is a powerful process. You can do it just to cure your backache, or you can use it to transform yourself absolutely. It has that potential. - Sadhguru

Time to learn: 6-8 hrs

Time to practice: ~30 min

Yogasanas

Yogasanas are a set of powerful postures through which one can elevate one's consciousness and manipulate energies.

Practicing asanas with the necessary dedication will transform your physiological, psychological, and energy structure, thus facilitating an energetic, creative, and blissful life. - Sadhguru

Time to learn: 10-12 hrs

Time to practice: 1-1.5 hrs

Bhuta Shuddhi

Bhuta Shuddhi means "purification of the five elements" (i.e. earth, water, fire, air and space) which gets to root of imbalances in the body that lead to disease. This simple practice which you can do daily reorganizes your system on the elemental level.

Bhuta Shuddhi is about removing everything that you have built up so that the Creator's creation will rise and shine within you. - Sadhguru

Time to learn: 1.5 hrs

Time to practice: 15 min
