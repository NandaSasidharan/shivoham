    Classical Hatha Yoga in Switzerland

    Yogic practices or Sadhana is a way of applying yourself fully. To engage your body, mind, emotions, & energies so that you are flexible on all levels. So that you are not a hurdle in your own life. To that end, we offer Hatha Yoga practices & meditations structured by Sadhguru, a yogi, mystic, and visionary.

    A unique opportunity!

    Classical Hatha Yoga programs we offer are an unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years.

    They are not taught merely for physical fitness and strength. They are a holistic process of naturally achieving a certain mastery over the body and mind, so as to come to a state of health, joy, and bliss.

    Yoga programs we offer

    Upa-Yoga

    A simple yet powerful set of 10 practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, bringing ease to the whole system.

    Surya Shakti

    Energize the system to a different dimension. This 18-step process helps to strengthen the ligaments that hold the skeletal and muscular structure together.

    Angamardana

    A series of 31 dynamic processes, rooted in yoga, to invigorate the body and reach peak physical fitness and mental health.

    Surya Kriya

    A potent 21-step yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health and inner wellbeing.

    Yogasanas

    Yogasanas are a set of powerful postures through which one can elevate one's consciousness and manipulate energies.

    Bhutha Shuddhi

    Bhuta Shuddhi means purification of the five elements, i.e. earth, water, fire, air and space, which gets to root of imbalances in the body that lead to disease.
