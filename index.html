    Why Classical Hatha Yoga?

    For those who have not yet tried Hatha Yoga, they may seem like difficult postures. Those who have tried it with some involvement know how it relaxes the system. Those who regularly keep up the practices know the health benefits of Hatha Yoga. Now if you practice it with the necessary precision and awareness, and with a sense of offering, it becomes a live process that gradually grows within you and blossoms to make life a joyful experience.

    Yoga as a technology to explore the full depth and dimension of being human originated some 15,000 years ago. This technology survived these many millennia simply due to its efficacy. Sadhguru's insights and guidance make this technology available to modern times without losing its essence. The word 'classical' in our Hatha Yoga offering indicates that we make no compromise in this original intention.

    The journey of yoga is subjective. It is different for each person. We can only take a step from where we are. Therefore, we offer simple and effective Upa-Yoga practices that anyone can easily start with. If you are willing, we also offer practices that energize and invigorate the system and practices that instil stillness and silence.
