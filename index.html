The Practices

Six classical yogic forms

Each practice is a complete, self-contained system. Together they offer a progressive path — from activating the physical body to working with subtler dimensions of energy and consciousness. No equipment or prior experience needed.

Foundation · Beginners

Upa-Yoga

Ten simple yet powerful practices that activate the joints, muscles and energy system, releasing accumulated tensions and bringing ease to the whole system.

4-5 hrs to learn~30 min practice
Flexibility · Fitness

Angamardana

A series of 31 processes to invigorate the body and cultivate peak physical and mental fitness — entirely without equipment.

Angamardana is a beautiful way of preparing the human mechanism for a higher possibility. Above all, it is one of the simplest and most powerful ways to gain fitness because it does not need any equipment or outside support. — Sadhguru
11-14 hrs to learn~40 min practice
Strength · Ligaments

Surya Shakti

An 18-step sun-salutation sequence designed to energise the system and strengthen the ligaments that bind the skeletal and muscular structure.

3-4 hrs to learn1-3 min/cycle
Energy · Wellbeing

Surya Kriya

A potent 21-step yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health and inner wellbeing — with profound cumulative effects.

One should approach Surya Kriya with devotion and reverence - it is a powerful process. You can do it just to cure your backache, or you can use it to transform yourself absolutely. It has that potential. — Sadhguru
6-8 hrs to learn~20 min/cycle
Postures · Awareness

Yogasanas

A classical set of powerful postures through which one can elevate consciousness and work with the body's energy structure.

10-12 hrs to learn1-1.5 hrs practice
Cleansing · Elements

Bhuta Shuddhi

A daily practice of purifying the five elements within the body — working at the root of imbalances that lead to disease.

Bhuta Shuddhi is about removing everything that you have built up so that the Creator's creation will rise and shine within you. — Sadhguru
1.5 hrs to learn10 min practice
