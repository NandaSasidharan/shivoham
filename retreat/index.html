Isha Classical Hatha Yoga and Ayurvedic Food

For enhanced energy, balance, well-being, and joy. Relax in and explore the valley. Yogic practices and food recipes to take home.

"Hatha Yoga in its original form is not an exercise form to gain fitness or to lose weight but a methodology for a human being to flower into an ultimate possibility" - Sadhguru

In these four days, we want to offer you a glimpse of yoga, the taste of ayurvedic food, and a drop of spirituality.

We will learn simple yet effective Upa-Yoga practices, a dynamic form of the Sun Salutation known as Surya Shakti, Pranayama, Meditation and more. All the processes are taught in such a way that you are enabled to practice them on your own after the retreat to deepen the practice from the comfort of your home and experience the full benefits.

We will also relish Ayurvedic food as well as explore the preparation and consumption of food from an Ayurvedic perspective. We will also have sessions where we look at how to enhance the quality of your sleep.

In addition:

  • Explore the various aspects of Yoga with insightful videos of Sadhguru.
  • Connect with Earth with barefoot hikes in the valley while marvelling at the high mountains.
  • Take a refreshing bath in the natural pool of the Kientalerhof.
  • Or a little time off to relax with Ayurvedic massages and a steaming Sauna (at additional cost).

Upa-Yoga

Upa-yoga is a simple yet powerful system of exercise that activates the joints, muscles, and energy system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body's mechanics, Upa-yoga dispels inertia in the body's energy and brings ease to the whole system.

  • Relieves physical stress and tiredness
  • Strengthens the joints and muscles
  • Rejuvenates the body after periods of inactivity
  • Negates the effects of jetlag and long travel
Know more

Surya Shakti

When we say Surya, we are talking about the Sun which is the basic powerhouse of the planet. Life on the planet is solar powered, including human system. Surya Shakti is a way of using that energy. It does wonders for your health, as well as your physical and mental wellbeing.

  • Brings about physical fitness and overall wellbeing
  • Increases mental alertness and focus
  • Creates a basis to move into higher states of energy
  • Increases physical strength & stamina
  • Increases energy levels
