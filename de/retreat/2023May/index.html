Yoga & Mountains Immersion Retreat

Explore Isha Classical Hatha Yoga
during Pfingsten holidays (26-29th May 2023) in Kiental

In collaboration with Isha Hatha Yoga Teachers Mirjam Joyce(yogaaa.ch) & Merlijn

Immerse yourself in Isha Classical Hatha Yoga surrounded by the iconic Swiss mountains in Kiental, Berner Oberland.

"Hatha Yoga in its original form is not an exercise form to gain fitness or to lose weight but a methodology for a human being to flower into an ultimate possibility" - Sadhguru

During these 4 days, we want to offer you a glimpse of Yoga. You can learn simple yet effective Upa-Yoga practices and a powerful set of 21 Yogasanas. You will also learn Pranayama, Meditation and more. All the practices are taught in such a way that you are empowered to do them on your own after the retreat as life-transforming tools.

  • Explore the various aspects of Yoga with insightful videos of Sadhguru.
  • Experience the magnitude of the surrounding mountains with a barefoot hike and connect with our mother earth.
  • Connect with other participants and Isha Hatha Teachers.
  • Get in touch with the 5 elements, enjoy the breathtaking view of the night sky, and let your body & mind rejuvenate from the hectic pace of the modern world.
  • Enjoy delicious, nourishing, and energising pranic food and learn how food can assist you in your life.
  • Take a refreshing bath in the natural pool of the Kientalerhof and relax in the steaming sauna.

Upa-Yoga

Upa-yoga is a simple yet powerful system of exercise that activates the joints, muscles, and energy system. Based on a sophisticated understanding of the body's mechanics, Upa-yoga dispels inertia in the body's energy and brings ease to the whole system. It relieves physical stress and tiredness.

  • Relieves physical stress and tiredness
  • Strengthens the joints and muscles
  • Rejuvenates the body after periods of inactivity
  • Negates the effects of jetlag and long travel
Know more

Yogasanas

Hatha Yoga is offered as a set of 21 powerful postures, or yogasanas, to enable the system to sustain higher dimensions of energy. This profound science enhances how one thinks, feels, and experiences life. Isha's program requires no special physical agility or previous experience of yoga. It is not merely physical exercise, but enables a person to flower into his ultimate potential; to naturally achieve a state of health, joy, and bliss.

  • Relief of chronic health conditions
  • Evolution of body and mind towards a higher possibility
  • Stabilization of the body, mind, and energy system
  • Deceleration of the aging process
